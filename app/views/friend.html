Neljä käärmettä liukuu

Four snakes sliding in a pit

liukuu kuopassa

not to eat

ei syödäkseen

not to love

but only to

ei rakastaakseen

vaan vain

slither

Here the freedom is yours and you are in charge of your experience. You may gain new ways of thinking, begin to question things and detach yourself from the mundane. In the labyrinth you will face the world alone and the choices are yours.

Experiential circus arts production, a labyrinth under the ground.
