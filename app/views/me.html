We are a group of artists from different fields of art: circus, fine art, music, light and other medias. We build experimental labyrinths. Our performances combine all these art forms, creating worlds that compete with 3D-movies and video games.

Our work contemplates personal freedom and an individual’s choices. In the labyrinth, the viewer is lost in time, space and dimension and in their head. The goal is to trigger emotions and thoughts, and also to fade out the ”fourth wall” between the performer and the viewer, by bringing them together on the same level, able to make contact if the viewer so desires.

We aim to give the viewer the ultimate experience of performance art. The audience gets absorbed in the surreal world of the human psyche one by one, as they enter the labyrinth alone. We are interested in individuals inside groups. Can peoples’ reactions and choices be affected through art?

Here the freedom is yours and you are in charge of your experience. You may gain new ways of thinking, begin to question things and detach yourself from the mundane. In the labyrinth you will face the world alone and the choices are yours. You will get to observe the natural, yet absurd cycles of life close up.
